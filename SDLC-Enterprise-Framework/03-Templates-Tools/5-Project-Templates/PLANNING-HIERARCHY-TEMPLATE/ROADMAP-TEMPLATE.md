## Purpose

This template provides a standard structure for 12-month product roadmaps. Use this for strategic planning at the highest level of the 4-Level Planning Hierarchy.

**Position in Hierarchy**: Roadmap → Phase → Sprint → Backlog

## Tier-Specific Guidelines

### LITE Tier
- **Not Required** - Use simple version tracking (v1.0, v1.1)

### STANDARD Tier
- **Optional** - Simplified roadmap (6 months, quarterly milestones only)

### PROFESSIONAL Tier
- **Recommended** - Full 12-month roadmap with quarterly milestones

### ENTERPRISE Tier
- **Mandatory** - Full roadmap + resource allocation + risk register

---

## Checklist

Before finalizing Roadmap, verify:

- [ ] Vision statement is clear and inspiring
- [ ] Strategic goals have measurable success metrics
- [ ] Quarterly milestones are realistic
- [ ] Dependencies are identified
- [ ] Resource allocation aligns with milestones
- [ ] Quality gates are mapped to timeline
- [ ] Stakeholders are identified
